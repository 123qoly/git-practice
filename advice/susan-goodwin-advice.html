Hi, my name is Susan and I have some advice for you!

Issues and errors are a gold mine.

You will learn so much by troubleshooting issues that come up! Every problem you solve will add to your knowledge base in a big way.

Ask for help.

Don't spend too long troubleshooting something before asking for help. Often it can be a simple typo that someone else may spot in a minute that you haven't seen while looking at it for an hour.
