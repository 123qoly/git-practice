Hi, my name is Brent and I have some advice for you!

Keep doing it --daily!

Learning something new feels unnatural. It's hard. Like learning to walk. The way forward is to keep doing it over and over, every single day, even just a little, and it will slowly start to feel natural. Just do it every day even if it feels pointless, eventually it won't.
