Hi, my name is Amanda and I have some advice for you!

Network and learn from other web developers!!

If you do not already have social media accounts, get them! Networking with other web developers will provide you with knowledge you cannot always get from the Internet or books. Learning from others experiences and knowledge will have a profound effect on your development in web design.

Never fear, Google is near!

Google is a huge reference for any and all coding, styling, and troubleshooting questions you may have or situations you may encounter. Google is your friend, don't forget that!

Skillcrush is where it is at!

I've been able to learn a lot from Skillcrush so far. They have made it easy to understand the material and learn best practices for when I venture into the world to make my own way in the crazy world of web development. Find your sweet spot for learning the language and type of development you are most interested in and stick with it. DON'T GIVE UP!
